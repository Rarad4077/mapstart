Chinotech is a pioneer brand in providing smart city and smart building solutions. It is owned by NTK (Hong Kong) Electronic Technology Limited, which is a subsidiary of NTK Holdings. NTK was found in 1988 and has since then established itself as a leading thermal solution provider in the semiconductor industry. Chinotech embraces smart city and smart building solutions. Our mission is to integrate and innovate on Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) into our surroundings, in order to benefits the occupants of the Cities and Buildings. More importantly, Chinotech is motivated to create a sustainable environment through the innovation and integration of ICTs.
