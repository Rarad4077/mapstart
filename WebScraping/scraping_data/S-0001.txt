App Building
We have been building applications both for iOS and Android platforms for more than 10 years, from entertainment to banking services applications. And by building apps based on your needs, we also help you to streamline and expand your business, and expose to the new clients. To make out the most of the project, we will consult the clients for the details they need, giving out the optimal solution for both parties. Not only the technical details and features, you can also leave the design to our hands, we will ensure the design is attractive and up-to-date.
Artificial Intelligence
We work with the latest technologies, and are experienced in applying A.I. as business solutions. With our dedicated A.I. development team, we have delivered various A.I. projects. Examples include smart adviser for business inquiry, check-in using facial recognition, venue heat map for promotional optimization, industrial equipment failure warning system etc. Our team are experienced in customizing different machine learning algorithm for our business clients, and help them use data and automation to build a competitive edge in their markets.
Web Building
A memorable and attractive website leaves a special impression for the guests. We provide not only fully responsive web services and development but also a well-mannered communication. We regularly have a conversation with our clients to ensure their needs are met, followed up by homepage proposal to give them the highlight features they are looking for afterward. Ensuring our clients’ requirement before they commit to any project is our value, and we keep providing the best services by giving progress track in a timely manner about the development.
Customer Relationship Management System (CRM)
We help our clients not just with technology but to earn more money. Every business wants to keep their clients loyal, increase purchases from existing clients, and to acquire new customers. We partner with Salesforce, the largest CRM provider, to offer an effective sales solution. CRM helps store customer information and contacts, keeps track of customer activity like purchase history, frequency and demographics. With easy setup, our clients can create sales campaigns that are personal, relevant, and up to date. How effective this can drive sales? Salesforce customers report 37% more sales revenue, 45% higher customer satisfaction, 43% better marketing ROI.
Our Vision
Communication
We regard communication as the most fundamental principal in each of our work. A good communication can express not only ideas but also feelings. It helps us to understand our clients even more. Only through mutual understanding between us and our clients, we can deliver the best result.
Technology Integration
Technology is undeniably a part of our life. It comes in many forms, not only the Internet and its subsidiary but also the other systems. Currently, we are integrating few numbers of them to our works, to enhance the mankind living quality.
