ABOUT RV
Founded in August of 2015, RV Automation Technology Company Limited (RV) put the focus on developing people centric robotic applications in logistics, manufacturing, healthcare, retailing and education sectors. 
OUR MISSION
Combining the automation technologies and real-life robotic applications, we aim to provide a comprehensive and one-stop solution and thus address the business pain point in lower cost but higher effectiveness and efficiency.
